Lake Como is one of the most beautiful places in Italy and in the world. With its deep blue waters, noble villas, medieval villages and the backdrop of the Alps, it enchants millions of visitors every year. In this guide we take you through the must-see spots.

## Villa del Balbianello – Lenno

Widely regarded as the most beautiful villa on the lake, Villa del Balbianello stands on a wooded promontory in Lenno. Its terraced garden overlooking the water is a place of rare beauty, made even more famous as a filming location for **007 Casino Royale** and **Star Wars: Episode II**.

The most stunning view is from the water: sailing past the villa you can appreciate its privileged position and its perfect harmony with the surrounding nature.

## Bellagio – The Pearl of the Lake

Bellagio is the symbol of Lake Como. Perched on the headland that divides the two branches of the lake, it has a charming historic centre with cobblestone alleys, artisan shops and lakeside restaurants. Don't miss **Villa Serbelloni** and its panoramic gardens.

The best way to arrive? By boat, of course. Enjoying the cruise and gliding into Bellagio from the lake has a magic that no ferry can match.

## Varenna – The Romantic Village

Varenna, on the eastern shore, is perhaps the most romantic village on the lake. Its lakeside steps, the **Castello di Vezio**, the flower-lined promenade and the Lovers' Walk make it an unmissable stop. From the water it looks like a painting.

## Villa Carlotta – Tremezzo

In Tremezzo you'll find Villa Carlotta, famous for its extraordinary botanical garden. In spring, when the rhododendrons and azaleas are in bloom, it becomes a fairytale garden. The museum inside holds works by Canova and Thorvaldsen.

## The Historic Centre of Como

The city of Como has a beautiful medieval heart, with its **Cathedral**, the **Broletto** and Roman walls still visible. It's worth spending at least half a day here before or after your boat tour.
